Link, the Rupees Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four rupees below. By clicking on a rupee you will add a specific amount of rupees to your wallet.

You win the game by matching your total amount of rupees to the random number, you lose the game if your total amount of rupees goes above the random number.

The value of each rupee is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each rupee.
